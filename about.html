QUALITY YOU FEEL.

S-Connect was founded in 2018 by Myana Srinivas, a first-generation entrepreneur whose journey began long before the brand name existed. For nearly 15 years, Myana ran a mobile repair shop first known as Ideal Communications and later rebranded as Santhoshini Communications — a name that became synonymous with trust, honest service, and deep technical expertise in Hyderabad's mobile accessories market.

Through those years of hands-on repair work, he earned the loyalty of thousands of customers who returned again and again — not just for fixes, but for genuine guidance on what accessories actually work. That ground-level experience taught him one thing clearly: quality mobile accessories shouldn't be a luxury.

In 2018, Myana took a defining step — transforming years of industry knowledge into a manufacturing venture. Under the Made in India label, S-Connect began producing its own line of mobile accessories: charging adapters, data cables, power banks, earphones, and more. Every product carried the same promise he had built his reputation on — reliability you can feel, at a price that makes sense.

Today, SConnect stands as a brand built not in a boardroom, but on the workshop floor — shaped by real customers, real problems, and a founder who never stopped listening.
